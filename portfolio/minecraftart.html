For this project, I made a slice of cake in Minecraft.


This sculpture is a slice of cake which represents my childhood. When I was a child, cake felt like the most important part of my birthday, and I would get so picky about the flavor. If I didn’t get exactly what I wanted—sometimes even hoping for ice cream cake. We would always end up with huge cakes that barely got finished since my family wasn’t big on sweets. Nowadays, I’d rather just have a single slice for my birthday, because it’s enough to satisfy me and feels more meaningful than having an oversized cake we won’t eat! Plus less money wasted.
